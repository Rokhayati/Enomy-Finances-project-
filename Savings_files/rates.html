Rate Factors

Several factors can influence the actual rates you receive on your investments:

  • Market Conditions: Prevailing economic conditions and market performance significantly impact investment returns.
  • Investment Amount: Larger investments may qualify for preferential rates in some cases.
  • Investment Duration: Longer investment periods typically offer better potential for higher returns.
  • Risk Tolerance: Different risk profiles correspond to different potential return ranges.
  • Asset Allocation: The mix of assets in your portfolio affects both risk and potential returns.
